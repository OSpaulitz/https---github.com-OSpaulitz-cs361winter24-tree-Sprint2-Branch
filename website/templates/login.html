About Meal Ticket, Feb 12, 2024

What is this web application all about?

We started Meal Ticket for the person who doesn't have a lot of time to cook and who doesn't want to spend an arm and a leg for takeout. These are simple, affordable meals for the busy student, parent, adult low on time. We have tried to keep every recipe as straight forward as possible so you can get back to what you love doing.

Take A Look Around, Feb 12, 2024

What will you do for me?

We have a variety of sample recipes that you can browse to get an idea if this is right for you, or right for right now. Easy to Login and keep you favorite recipes on hand. There is even a place for you to rate and make notes on a recipe. Did you change out some ingredients? Did your stove work slower than the recipe state? Keep track of all of that to fit your needs. More recipes are available to registered users.
