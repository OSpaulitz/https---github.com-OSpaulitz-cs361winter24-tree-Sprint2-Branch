Albondiga Soup

Dinner

Ingredients:1 pound of ground beef, half a head of cabbage, bag of baby carrots, 1 can of corn, 1 pound of small fingerling potatoes, 2 zucchinis. 2 tablespoons of Oregano, 2 tablespoons of yerba buena, 2 handfuls of long grain rice, 3 tablespoons of powdered chicken bullion, garlic salt to taste.

Instruction: Bring 6 cups of water to boil on the stove. Combine ground beef and list of herbs along with the uncooked rice. Roll into small bite sized meatballs. Place meatballs into boiling water. Chop vegetables as desired. Add after 10 minutes. Continue for another 10 minutes. What you have is a surprisingly flavorful soup that's a crowd pleaser.
