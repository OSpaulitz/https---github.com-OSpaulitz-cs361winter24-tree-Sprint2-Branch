Summer Soup

Dinner

Ingredients: 3 lbs of tomatoes, 2 cans of white beans, handful of basil, 8 oz of mozzerella cheese, parmesean cheese, salt and pepper.

Instructions: Dice tomatoes and pour into a pot. Place pot over a low flame. Drain and rinse beans and pour into pot. Cube mozzerella cheese and sprinkle parmesean. Salt and Pepper to taste. As soon as it comes to a simmer turn it off. What you'll have is a light fresh dinner perfect for a hot summer night.
